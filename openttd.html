OpenTTD Server "Otherland"

This is not the usual goal server you might find. The goals are:

  • Be on top of the company league table.
  • Have a beautiful and realistic looking map.

But to get there, the settings are set to make it hard for everyone.

Besides the multiplayer rules you can find at wiki.openttd.org/Multiplayer_Rules, following rules apply on this server:

  1. No spaghetti networks! Try to find an esthetically way to build your rails/roads.
  2. Be realistic! Use at least 80% of the train length for curves whenever possible. No 45°-45° curves!
  3. Let the towns grow naturally, don't build road-squares! Help the growth with bus/tram stations and cargo deliveries!
  4. Don't be lazy! Use the right station for the right cargo! Close your terminus stations with buffers!
  5. Be fair! Before taking cargo from an already served industry, consult with your competitor.

So if you're convinced, look for "Otherland" in the server list! The password is otherland.
